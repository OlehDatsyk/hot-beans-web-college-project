Meet the Team

Oleh: Full Stack Web Developer

Started as a trainee. Expert in HTML/CSS.

Oleh: Bio & Skills

Oleh joined Hot Beans six months ago after earning his Level 3 Extended Diploma in IT. Initially concerned about his lack of industry experience, his disciplined work ethic and commitment to daily coding sprints allowed him to master the company’s tech stack in record time. He is known for his meticulous attention to detail and his ability to bridge the gap between complex back-end logic and sleek front-end design using HTML and CSS.

Jenna: Junior Developer

Transitioning from a fast-paced retail environment, Jenna brought a high-energy work ethic and exceptional problem-solving skills to the team. She holds a Certification in JavaScript Algorithms and Data Structures, which she completed through self-study before joining us.
