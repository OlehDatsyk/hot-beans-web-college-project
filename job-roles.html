Job Roles

Junior Web Developer

We seek a highly driven person to join our team. The details for the Junior Web Developer position are as follows:

Category Details
Salary £42,000 - £45,000 per year (depending on experience)
Hours 37.5 hours per week (Monday to Friday, 9:00 - 17:30)
Location Hot Beans Office (with 2 days working from home)
  • Creating clean code with HTML, CSS and a little bit of JavaScript.
  • Testing websites to find and fix bugs.
  • Collaborating with the design team to ensure good appearance of websites.
  • Renovating client websites.
  • Proficiency in HTML5, CSS3, and basic JavaScript. Knowledge of Git, Python, Unity/C# is a plus.
  • Excellent communication and willingness to study.
  • Ability to work well in a team.
  • 5 GCSEs at grade 4/C, including Maths and English, BTEC IT Level 3 or equivalent.

Full Stack Web Developer

It is a position that requires an individual to work on the front and back side of a web site. It is an advanced position of a talented developer:

Category Details
Salary £68,000 - £102,000 per year.
Hours 37.5 hours per week (Monday to Friday, 9:00 - 17:30)
Location Hot Beans Office (with 2 days working from home)
  • Making up and supporting dynamic websites.
  • Ensuring web pages operate more efficiently and effectively.
  • Working closely with designers and senior developers.
  • Good familiarity with HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript.
  • Knowledge of React or Vue would be beneficial.
  • Learning to use Git as a version control.
  • 5 GCSEs grade 4/C (including Maths and English).
  • A-Level or BTEC IT Level 3 Qualification (DDD - D*D*D*) or equivalent experience in web development.

This role looks at the aspects of the site, which the users view and interact with. It suits well the creative eye:

Category Details
Salary £44,000 - £48,000 per year.
Hours 37.5 hours per week (Monday to Friday, 9:00 - 17:30)
Location Hot Beans Office (with 2 days working from home)
  • Creating friendly interfaces and developing them.

  • Enhancing the user experience of multiple web browsers and devices.

  • A-Level or BTEC IT Level 3 Qualification (DDD - D*D*D*) or equivalent experience in web development.

Backend Developer

This is a position on the behind-the-scenes rationale i.e. servers and databases. It is superb when someone is fond of reason and statistics:

Category Details
Salary £25,000 - £29,000 per year.
Hours 37.5 hours per week (Monday to Friday, 9:00 - 17:30)
Location Hot Beans Office (with 2 days working from home)

  • 5 GCSEs grade 4/C (including Maths and English).
